Importance of Fluid Tracking for Heart Patients

Fluid tracking is crucial for heart patients as it helps manage hydration levels, preventing both dehydration and fluid overload. Proper fluid balance is essential for maintaining heart function and preventing complications such as swelling, shortness of breath, and hypertension.

By regularly monitoring fluid intake, patients can better manage their condition, adhere to dietary restrictions, and improve their overall health outcomes. The Fluid Tracking app enables users to log their daily fluid consumption easily, ensuring they stay within recommended limits and adjust as necessary based on their individual needs.
